 Revised "Institutional Trust" Color Palette
This palette moves away from "Cyberpunk Blue" toward "Establishment Blue" and "Heritage Accents" to signal longevity and compliance.
Role	Color	Hex Code	Purpose
Primary (60%)	Deep Navy	#001F3F	The foundation of trust and authority.
Secondary (30%)	Ivory / Off-White	#F8F9FA	For a clean, high-contrast background that is easy on the eyes.
Accent (10%)	British Racing Green	#004225	Subtly signals "UK Market" and financial growth.
Call to Action	Muted Gold	#C5A059	High-contrast button color that feels premium, not "salesy".

3. Immediate Technical "Fixes"
Typography: Switch to a professional pairing like Playfair Display (Headings) and Inter (Body). This signals a mix of "Established Authority" and "Modern Tech."
Button Treatment: Give your button a slight shadow or border-radius (4px) to make it feel like a "physical" high-quality tool rather than just text on a screen.
Copy Density: Move your long text list ("Strategic Fractional COO/CRO | Scaling B2B Tech...") into the Footer or as a separate "About the Methodology" section.
